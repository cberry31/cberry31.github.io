WANTS

This impactful project was developed in collaboration with the Washington Sheriffs and jails, aiming to streamline and enhance the process of transporting individuals. Working within a close-knit team, my primary responsibility was crafting the core functionality for the web application. Employing a React frontend and a Node backend seamlessly connected to a MySQL Database, I delved into the intricacies of Node, React, and JavaScript. The project presented a valuable learning opportunity, allowing me to expand my skill set and gain a deeper understanding of the multifaceted functionalities of these technologies. My contributions to the project included the creation of key features such as the enter transport, search, and the transport list. Notably, the project's complexity was heightened by the diverse approaches required to formulate MySQL queries for various types of searches, showcasing my adaptability and problem-solving skills. This venture marked my inaugural foray into professional collaboration with members of the Washington Jail System. It was a rewarding experience to actively engage with their ideas, translating concepts into tangible solutions within the framework of this project. The knowledge and insights gained from this endeavor have become foundational in shaping my approach to subsequent professional challenges.

INVY

Navigating the intricacies of this assignment marked my inaugural exposure to MongoDB, and the experience proved to be a transformative learning opportunity. My role in bridging the gap between the frontend and MongoDB not only expanded my technical acumen but also provided valuable insights into the nuances of database connectivity using Node.js. In essence, this project served as a milestone in my journey, offering a hands-on exploration of MongoDB and solidifying my proficiency in integrating diverse components to create cohesive and functional web applications. The lessons learned from this experience have since become integral to my approach in handling subsequent development challenges.
